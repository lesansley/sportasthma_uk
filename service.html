What can Sport Asthma offer you?

At Sport Asthma we offer eucapnic voluntary hyperpnea and bronchodilator challenge testing for athletes, which are the diagnostic tests for exercise-induced asthma recommended by the International Olympic Committee (IOC).
We have developed a mobile EVH testing kit that allows us to conduct testing either in a laboratory or in the field at training facilities. Results are immediate and the report generated from the challenge can be included in a TUE application and presented to a physician for treatment consideration.
